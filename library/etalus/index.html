Ice Armor generated f64 on ice. Ice Armor is broken after 40% of damage is taken or is removed immediately upon being parried. Etalus must also be placed in hitstun for armour to break from an attack, meaning moves like Ori's Spirit Flame or Maypul's seed are unable to break armor despite dealing damage. Ice cannot disappear while Etalus is using Neutral Special.
