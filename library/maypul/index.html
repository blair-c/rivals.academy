Tether

StartupProjectile Invuln.EndlagCancellableFAF
Initial: 9
Tether: 5		Travel Time (15-)7Specials, except up special: Endlag 1-7
Aerials/Airdodge (Target in Hitstun, Tether left/right): Endlag 1-7
8 (Post Travel Time)

Tether's travel time scales with distance, the direction you travel to (Above, In Front, Behind) and whether the marked player is in hitstun or not. Tether travel time is 3 times faster when the marked player is in hitstun and a minimum 5 frames slower when they are not. Hitstun distinction registered at the start of tether. Cancelling tether endlag into Up Air will double the knockback scaling of the move's multi-hits. Maypul has the ability to tether to any character that has been wrapped as if they are marked and in hitstun.

Place Lily

StartupEndlagFAFLifetimeCooldown

Lily: 45		333480f (75-554)

Lily will spit an active seed up in the air upon eating it. If this seed does not collide with another character, object or platform it will then be swallowed by Lily again, resetting her lifetime duration a second time before disappearing. Maypul can place Lily again during Lily's despawn animation, as well as during the hitpause of Lily's bite.
