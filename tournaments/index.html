Tournaments

Free online brackets held in the Rivals Academy Discord and our sister server, Rivals Recess

Rivals Academy Brackets Copied link!

  • Rivals Amateur Series

    Thursdays @ 8:00 PM ET (weekly)

    The community’s most longstanding amateur bracket, in which each week’s winner “graduates” and is banned from future RAS events, seen as a rite of passage.

  • Rivals Amateur Series (EU)

    Saturdays @ 8:30 PM CEST (weekly)

    Europe’s take on the RAS format, with a seasonal ban format for three-time winners. Joining the EU Rivals Discord is required to enter this event.

  • Go to School

    Wednesdays @ 8:00 PM ET (weekly)

    Adopted from the long-running “Go-to-Sleep” tournament series, GTS is Academy’s weekly open bracket open to all skill levels. Enter to compete with Rivals’ best.

Rivals Recess Brackets Copied link!

  • Rivals Recess Circuit

    Sundays @ 7:00 PM ET (weekly)

    Amateur bracket with a skill cap slightly below RAS’s, a seasonal structure + invitational, and round robin pools giving everyone a chance to squeeze in many sets.

  • SSSS

    Tuesdays @ 8:00 PM ET (weekly)

    Recess’ weekly open bracket, with a substantial amount of top level representation and making up the bulk of Rivals’ active online scene alongside GTS.

  • The Kingpin

    Saturdays @ 4:00 PM ET (monthly)

    Monthly open series boasting the largest prize pots, entrant counts, and stakes. Features an extended stream to cover the action.
